Founder

 

Posted on 16th August, 2019

There was a man called Ray Kroc, who started working with a company at the age of 52, as a franchise agent and left the job of salesman of milkshake machine. He loved the company so much that he could able to buy it from its founders even after facing loss at the beginning. 

How did he do it?

How does a man at the age of 52 can enter a business and turns it into a multi-million dollar business?

One word: Persistence!

Ray Kroc the owner and CEO of the Mc Donald’s Corporation was persistent and determined towards what he was doing. He mortgage his only house. The stakes were high, so does his determination.

At an age near to retirement, he had the passion and the will to do something revolutionary.

All his life he was selling something or the other of which sometimes brought him people’s mocking. Still, he never stopped, continued dreaming to achieve success and had a faith in himself. He never lose hope. All this while, it was his persistence that dragged him towards success. He was a man who not only want himself to grow but also want others to grow and take the path of progress. He looked for other hustlers like him to join him. He believed that we all are one step away from the opportunity. Opportunity is what we need along with hard work and persistence to grow and achieve success.

But why did he bought that restaurant at first place? Why didn’t he even change its name?

Well, to know the reason behind it, you should watch the movie “Founder”, of an ordinary man with extraordinary determination, always looked out for an opportunity and how being persistent changed his life. It’s not just a movie for upcoming managers and future leaders, its more than that!
